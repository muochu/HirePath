Getting Started

HirePath Saver helps you track job listings as you browse, so you can focus on the application process without losing track of opportunities.

1

Log in to your HirePath account

Click the extension icon in your browser and log in with your HirePath credentials. If you don't have an account yet, you'll be directed to sign up.

2

Browse job listings

Visit job boards like LinkedIn, Indeed, or Glassdoor and navigate to job listings that interest you.

3

Save jobs with one click

When viewing a job listing, you'll see a "Save to HirePath" button. Click it to instantly save the job to your HirePath dashboard.

4

Track your applications

Visit your HirePath dashboard to see all saved jobs, update their status, add notes, and monitor your application progress.
